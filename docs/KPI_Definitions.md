## KPI Definitions


# Total Complaints
Definition:
Total number of complaint records.
Formula:
COUNT(Complaint ID)

# Timely Response %
Definition:
Percentage of complaints receiving timely responses.
Formula:
(Timely Responses / Total Complaints) * 100

# Complaint Growth Rate
Definition:
Monthly change in complaints.

# Top Complaint Product
Definition:
Product with highest complaint volume.

# Top Complaint Issue
Definition:
Issue with highest complaint volume.

# State Risk Score
Definition:
Ranking of states based on complaint count.
