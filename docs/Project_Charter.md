
## Consumer Complaint Intelligence & Risk Analytics Platform

# Project Overview

The project aims to analyze consumer complaints related to financial products and services.
The analysis will help management understand complaint patterns, customer issues, product risks, and response effectiveness.

# Business Problem
Financial institutions receive thousands of complaints through different channels.
Without proper analytics:

Customer pain points remain hidden
Product issues are not identified early
Response performance cannot be measured
Risk areas become difficult to track

# Project Goal
Build an analytics platform that:

Tracks complaints
Measures response performance
Identifies high-risk products
Analyzes customer issues
Supports decision-making
